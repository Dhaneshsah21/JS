About Aqua Sports

In Aqualis, sports aren't played on land — they flow with the tides. Citizens dive through shimmering arenas where light and water dance together. The games test not only strength but rhythm, balance, and harmony with the currents.

Current Racing

athletes glide through underwater rings, guided by glowing currents.

Bubble Sphere

a fast-paced team game played inside a giant floating bubble.

Coral Glide

surfers ride on crystal fins along bioluminescent coral waves.

Aqua Dance Combat

a graceful martial art combining motion, sound, and water ripples.
