About Lumina Isles

Lumina Isles are a chain of bioluminescent islands floating upon calm, glowing tides. Each night, the entire ocean lights up with purple and blue hues, creating a dreamlike world of soft light and peace.

Glow Bay

A peaceful bay where every wave glows with soft lavender light.

Star Lagoon

A lagoon reflecting the night sky — it's said stars fall here to rest.

Coral Moon

An island shaped like a crescent moon, covered in shimmering corals.

Lightspire Isle

A tall island where glowing plants send beams of light into the clouds.
