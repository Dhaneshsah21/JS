Aqua Festivals

Wave Festivals bring color and light to the seas of Aqualis.

About Festivals

Citizens celebrate through music, glowing lanterns, and coral dances. Each tide carries joy, unity, and the rhythm of the ocean's heart

Tide Glow Festival

When the twin moons align, the seas shimmer with glowing plankton. Citizens light floating lanterns that drift across the waves, symbolizing hope and unity.

Song of Currents

A musical celebration where seashell instruments echo through coral cities. The melodies rise and fall like waves, connecting every heart beneath the ocean.

Coral Bloom

Once every year, coral gardens burst into radiant colors overnight. People gather to honor the sea's renewal and decorate their homes with glowing shells.

Flame of the Deep

In the darkest trenches, fire corals ignite with natural light. Divers swim in patterns around them, marking the balance of darkness and life.
